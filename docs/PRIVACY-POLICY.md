# Privacy Policy – Rankset

**Last updated:** [Add date]

Rankset ("we", "our") operates the Rankset progressive web app. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information we collect

When you register, we collect: **name**, **father's name**, **competitive exam name**, **email address**, **phone number**, and **password** (stored in encrypted form). When you take tests, we store your **scores**, **attempts**, and **rank-related data**.

## How we use it

We use your data to:
- Provide the test and ranking service
- Show you your rank and leaderboards
- Send password reset emails (via our secure integration)
- Improve the app

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## Advertising

We may use **Google AdSense** to show ads. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to personalize ads. You can control ad preferences via [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com) and your browser settings.

## Data storage

Your data is stored on **MongoDB Atlas** (cloud database) and our app runs on **Render**. We use industry-standard practices to keep data secure.

## Your rights

You may request **access to**, **correction of**, or **deletion of** your data by contacting us. You can change your password and profile from within the app.

## Children

Our service is aimed at students preparing for competitive exams. If you are under 13, please use the service only with parental consent.

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes.
